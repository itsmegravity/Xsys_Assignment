Hi, I'm Arnav — a UI/UX designer with a passion for building clean, purposeful interfaces. I'm currently completing my final year at Mumbai University and will be joining XIMB for my MBA-BM program.

My work sits at the intersection of design thinking and technical curiosity. I believe good design should be invisible — it should simply feel right to the person using it.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me exploring audio hardware, gaming, or hunting for the perfect IEM.
